Company FAQ

Refund Policy:
Customers can request a refund within 30 days of purchase if the product has not been heavily used. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Support Hours:
Customer support is available Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM Eastern Time.

Account Access:
If a customer cannot access their account, they should reset their password first. If that fails, support can verify identity and escalate to the account recovery team.

Premium Plan:
The Premium plan includes priority support, advanced analytics, and unlimited document uploads.
